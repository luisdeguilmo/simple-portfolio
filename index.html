Projects

QR Attendance App

The QR Code Flag Ceremony Attendance Monitoring App is designed to streamline the attendance tracking process for faculty and staff during flag ceremonies. Administrators can easily generate unique QR codes for each department—CCIS, CTAS, and CCJ—that teaching and non-teaching staff can scan upon arrival. This ensures an efficient and organized attendance system, reducing manual errors and improving overall accountability.

7-segment Display

A 7-segment display is an electronic component commonly used for displaying numerical digits and some letters. It consists of seven individual segments (typically LEDs) arranged in a rectangular fashion." Each segment can be independently turned on or off to display numbers from 0 to 9 and some alphabetic characters.
